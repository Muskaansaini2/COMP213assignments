There's a significant distinction between purchasing and selling a home, each demanding customized responsibilities and support at every phase of the transaction.

If you are selling a house I will:

  • Effectively promote your property across various platforms including social media, online platforms, and multiple listing services.
  • Perform a comparative analysis of the market to showcase recent sales prices of similar properties in your vicinity.
  • Obtain support from additional experts to ensure precise paperwork completion within important timeframes.
  • Create a comprehensive profile for your property, detailing its current state, surroundings, and unique attributes.

If you are buying a house I will:

  • Provide you with information on the current state of the housing market.
  • Engage in discussions and analysis to identify the most suitable options that align with your budget and requirements.
  • Address any obstacles that may arise during the process of buying a new home.
  • Deliver top-notch service to ensure you feel at ease and confident throughout the process.
