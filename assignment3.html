MacBook Air

The MacBook Air is great for productivity tasks such as writing, editing and organizing documents.
It comes with a built-in keyboard and trackpad that makes it easy to type and navigate through
your files. Compared to a PC laptop with an Intel Core i7 processor, MacBook Air delivers upto
2x faster performance, upto 50 per cent faster web browsing and up to 50 per cent
longer battery life. It is an ideal option for most people who need a reliable
laptop for everyday tasks and a little bit more.

Advantages Of MacBook Air:

  • Lishtweight and thin
  • Longer battery life
  • Multiple colour options
  • M2 chip delivers excellent performance
